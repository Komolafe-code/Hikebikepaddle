How the App Works.

Enter or choose a destination landmark on the Homepage

Every journey has a destination. Travelling to a specific place requires a destination. Every User must fix a destination before setting out to carry out the activity of their choice. This helps record the user's progress.

Sign in to record Progress

Users can decide whether or not to record their progress. But in other to join the Hit the Street Challenge users have to get their milestones recorded and in this case, A unique account is allocated to the user, to help track progress and organize the streak points gathered while they are active outside.

Request for your unique Hit the street Card

Click the prompt that says "Request HTS card" once you've completed your account creation. It would be delivered to you in less than 24hrs, You need the card to accumulate points simply by connecting to the provided network services(WiFi) to ensure that you're active.

Ready to go

You're all ready to set out, ensure you lookout for the nearest Hit the street Square on the map to record your journey, Tap or hold your HTS card close to the square box, It will automatically add 20 points to your earnings.
